Talking points that start arguments (the good kind)

  • Enemies-to-lovers has lost demand share every single year since 2023 — while remaining the most-recommended trope in every author group.
  • Found family is the most durable trope in romance — three straight years holding its share while the conversation grew 8×.
  • The "write the big trope" era is measurably ending — the top ten tropes went from ~45% of reader demand to ~25% in three years.
  • Tropes don't die, they rotate lanes — found family is surging in MM and Historical right now while cooling in Western and Contemporary.
  • Demand moves before the charts do — reader-demand signals show a trend months before it reaches the also-boughts, which changes when (and what) to write.
