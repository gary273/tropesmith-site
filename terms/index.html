AI & accuracy

Maps are generated with help from AI models and large datasets. We work hard to make every Map accurate, useful, and fair, but:

  • Market data shifts. A trope hot this month may cool by next quarter.
  • AI can occasionally misclassify, miscount, or invent. We mitigate this with editorial passes and source-verification, but you should treat individual data points as evidence to weigh, not gospel.
  • Comp titles and trope rankings are recommendations based on patterns in the corpus, not promises about your book's reception.

Use Maps the way you'd use any other research tool: as one input into a creative decision that's still yours.
